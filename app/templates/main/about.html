Our Story

Redefining Luxury Travel Since 2010

Bek Tour was founded with a simple vision: to make extraordinary villa experiences accessible to discerning travelers around the world. What started as a small collection of handpicked villas has grown into a global platform with 500+ properties across 50 countries.

Every villa in our collection is personally vetted by our team to ensure it meets our strict standards of quality, comfort, and luxury. We go beyond just providing accommodation — we craft complete travel experiences.
